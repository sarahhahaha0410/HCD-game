How This Training Works

Before You Start

Follow the print workflow

The training follows the real lab order. Use the Hint button if you lose the next step.

  1. Enter the room with your ID card.
  2. Slice the model at the bench area.
  3. Collect the SD card and USB reader.
  4. Insert the SD card into the reader.
  5. Export the sliced file.
  6. Undock the reader, separate the SD card, insert it into the printer, start the print, then return the adapter.
